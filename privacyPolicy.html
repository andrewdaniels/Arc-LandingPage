DIGITAL ARC, LLC’S PRIVACY POLICY

Commitment to Privacy.

Digital Arc, LLC (“Digital Arc” or “We”) is committed to respecting your privacy while you use any of its services, products and websites.  This Privacy Policy applies to Digital Arc’s websites and any of its services and products that reference or incorporate this Privacy Policy. (Specific sites, services, and products may have additional specific privacy policies.) Submission of personal information to Digital Arc’s website or any of its Services constitutes agreement to this Privacy Policy and consent to the collection and disclosure of personal information according to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to inform you how Digital Arc will handle your personal information, how you can make changes to or correct your personal information, and what options you have concerning the collection, use, and access of your personal information.   

Definition of Personal Information.

For purposes of this Privacy Policy, “personal information” is information that can identify, relate to, describe, is reasonably capable of being associated with, or could reasonably be linked with a particular natural person or household directly or indirectly, or information that is specifically related to that identifiable natural person, such as a name, address, phone number, email address, identification number, online identifier, social media account information, payment card number, or other similar identifiers.  It can also include device identifiers, an internet protocol address, data and telemetry when such data is linked to a particular person’s device and can identify that person.

When you use Digital Arc websites, services, and products, we may collect data about you, including personal information.  In some cases, we utilize trusted third parties (such as a credit card processor) to collect personal information on our behalf.  We may also obtain personal information from trusted third parties.  

When we collect personal information from you, our intention is to implement a legitimate business purpose, such as:

  • Establishing an account;
  • Providing customer service and enhancing your experience in using our website, services, and products;
  • Processing payment transactions and orders for services or products;
  • Sending you marketing communications; 
  • Establishing and enabling services, products and features you have requested from Digital Arc;
  • For employment purposes.

Cookies and Other Technologies.

Digital Arc’s websites, services, and products may use cookies and other technologies such as pixel tags and web beacons.  We use these technologies for such reasons as helping us to understand which parts of our websites people have visited, to evaluate the effectiveness of web searches and advertisements, and to better understand the behavior of users of our website, services, and products.  Information collected by cookies and other technologies is generally considered nonpersonal information. However, to the extent that cookies or other technology gather information that can be linked to a particular person, then we treat such information as personal information under this privacy policy.   If you do not want us to use cookies in your browser, you can set your browser to reject cookies or to notify you when a website tries to use a cookie in your browser. If you choose to disable cookies in your browser, you can still visit our website and use our services and products; however, your ability to use some of our services may be affected.

Your California Privacy Rights.

In the last twelve months, Digital Arc may have collected, used, and shared, for business purposes, personal information as detailed in this privacy policy.  In addition, each category of data may be used by Digital Arc or shared with third parties as detailed in this privacy policy.  If you are a resident of California, you have the right to request access to and deletion of the information Digital Arc has about you.  You may submit such requests by writing us at Digital Arc, LLC, Attn: Privacy, 1723A Marsh Road, Suite 117, Wilmington, DE 19810, USA, or by emailing us at info@wearc.com.  Digital Arc will not sell your personal information without your consent.

Digital Arc is committed to not discriminating against those who exercise their California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) rights.  Among other commitments to those exercising their CCPA rights, Digital Arc will not deny access to its services and products, charge different rates, or provide a lower quality of service because an individual has exercised rights under the CCPA.  

Under California Civil Code § 1798.83, California residents may request from companies conducting business in California a list of all third parties to which the company has disclosed personal information during the preceding year for direct marketing purposes. Alternatively, this law provides that if the company has a privacy policy that gives either an opt-out or opt-in choice for use of your personal information by third parties for marketing purposes, the company may instead provide you with information on how to exercise your disclosure choice options.

Digital Arc is committed to providing this alternative option.  As part of this privacy policy, Digital Arc allows you to either opt-out or opt-in to the use of your personal information by third parties for direct marketing purposes.   Currently, Digital Arc does not sell your information to third parties for marketing purposes.  Digital Arc may utilize trusted third parties to assist with its own marketing purposes.  As detailed above, Digital Arc provides you with an option not to receive such marketing communications.
